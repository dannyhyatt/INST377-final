Frequently Asked Questions

What is SS2Cal?

SS2Cal is a tool that allows you to import any screenshot into a digital calendar. It uses AI to recognize the texts and layouts in your screenshots and convert them into a digital calendar format. This makes it easy to keep track of your events, appointments, and important dates without the hassle of manual entry.

Probably

How do I import a calendar?

To import a calendar, simply upload your screenshot using the upload button on the home page. SS2Cal will process the image and generate a digital calendar for you.
After the calendar is generated, you can download it as the standard .ics file format, which is compatible with most calendar applications.
Upload it to Google Calendar, Outlook, or any other calendar app that supports .ics files.
